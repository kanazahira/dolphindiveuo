Packages

Lovina Beach, Bali - IDR 200,000 per person

Details of Dolphin Watching Tour at Lovina Beach, Bali
  • Location: Lovina Beach is situated on the northern coast of Bali, approximately 10 km west of Singaraja.
  • Main Activity: Watching dolphins in their natural habitat. This is typically done by traditional boat in the early morning.
  • Duration: The tour usually lasts for 2-3 hours.
  • Time: This activity is best done in the morning around 6-8 am.
  • Guides: Tours are usually conducted with experienced local guides.
Notes: Prices may vary slightly depending on the tour operator and the season

Teluk Kiluan, Lampung - IDR 300,000 per person

Details of Dolphin Watching Tour at Teluk Kiluan, Lampung
  • Location: Teluk Kiluan is situated on the southern coast of Lampung, Indonesia, facing the Indian Ocean.
  • Main Activity: Dolphin watching in the natural habitat of dolphins by traditional wooden boats in the early morning.
  • Duration: The tour usually lasts for 2-3 hours.
  • Time: This activity is best done in the morning around 6-7 am.
  • Guides: Local guides familiar with the area and skilled in spotting dolphins will accompany the tour.
Notes: You can arrange private transport or take public transportation (bus or car) to reach Teluk Kiluan.

Banda Neira, Maluku - IDR 400,000 per person

Details of Dolphin Watching Tour at Banda Neira, Maluku
  • Location: Banda Neira is located in the Banda Islands, part of the Maluku Province in eastern Indonesia.
  • Main Activity: Watching dolphins in their natural habitat done by traditional boat in the early morning.
  • Duration: The tour usually lasts for 2-3 hours.
  • Time: This activity is best done in the morning around 6-8 am.
  • Guides: Tours are conducted with experienced local guides.
Notes: Prices may vary slightly depending on the tour operator and the season
